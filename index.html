    I developed and designed a ‘catalog-type website’ to advertise the products and services offered by 'Mercería el Botón', with options and catalog design, security system for users, ordering, and product updates.

  2. Web platform & assistance systemUniversidad Tecnológica de Tamaulipas Norte.
    09/2018 - 05/2019. Reynosa, Tamaulipas.

    I developed and designed the project of a ‘web platform for Student Assistance’, in conjunction with a desktop application that registered the fingerprints of the teaching staff and students for the 'Universidad Tecnológica de Tamaulipas Norte'.

  3. Web platformInstituto Tecnológico de Reynosa.

I temporarily taught face-to-face classes to the students of the facilities to share my knowledge about the technologies used in the development of Web Sites.
